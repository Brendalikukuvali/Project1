About us

Sejahtera aims to continue looking ahead in increasing its efforts in poverty eradication in Malaysia. We will strive to seek ways, not only to help participants earn additional income, enhance the educational experience and improve living conditions by equipping them with basic living necessities, but also hopefully give them the awareness, realization as well as opportunities to believe that poverty is not a condition they have been destined to live in.Our target group at Sejahtera is the low-income based on the national Poverty Line Index (PLI), the official indicator that measures poverty. Development Program

Recognising the need for a better quality of life for the poor in Pitas and Kota Marudu, Yayasan Sejahtera works hand-in-hand with Petroliam Nasional Berhad (PETRONAS) by providing basic needs and community services, as well as livelihood and community enhancement programmes through PETRONAS Planting Tomorrow project.Benefitting seven villages in Mukim Dandun, Pitas and three villages in Mukim Simpangan, Kota Marudu, the project started with the provision of water solutions to the villages, equipping the villages with gravity-fed water system, storage and filtration for a sustained clean water supply for the villagers that is nearer to their homes. Prior to the project, the villagers would need to walk for about one hour to the nearest stream to fetch water for daily use.

Enhancement program

Yayasan Sejahtera has responded to the challenge via the attempt to understand urban poverty and implementing programmes to support the low-income group through Community Enhancement Programme (CEP) PPR Sungai Bonus in Kuala Lumpur since July 2016

Women economic empowerment

In paving a path towards poverty eradication and inclusive economic growth, Yayasan Sejahtera embarked on a project to empower women in Kota Belud, Sabah to pursue economic activities in support of their family incomes.The community development programme in Kampung Pinggan Jaya is aimed to establish a gula apong business hub that can include the participation by the whole community at various levels to uplift the low income households and providing them the opportunity to leverage on the resources made available to them.
